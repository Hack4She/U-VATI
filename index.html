Levels Of Assessment

The Theory Round

It has questions based on theoratical knowledge of Data Structures and Algorithms.

Project Work

It has project questions that can involve puzzles and math questions to check the aptitude level of the candidate.

Communication Round

It is a round to check the communication skills of the candidate both in Eglish and Hindi. To check the fluency in speaking.

Code Rectification Round

A round to check whether the candidate is capable enough to think of different logics and not just one particular log they are aware of.

Team Project

To see whether the candidate has leadership qualities so that they can conduct and coordinate a class with full efficiency.

A Demo Teaching Round

To see whether the candidate is able and confident enough to convey their intellect to the students in a an understandable and friendly way.
